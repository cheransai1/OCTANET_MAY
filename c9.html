About Vizag Tourism

Experience the pristine beaches and vibrant culture of Visakhapatnam, where ancient temples meet modern amenities, offering a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary allure.

Locations

RK Beach

RK Beach, a jewel of Visakhapatnam, beckons with its golden sands, serene waves, and a bustling promenade adorned with vibrant shops and delectable street food stalls, making it a must-visit destination for both relaxation and exploration.

Kailasagiri

Perched atop a hill, Kailasagiri offers panoramic views of Visakhapatnam's coastline, adorned with lush greenery, charming sculptures, and a serene atmosphere, making it an idyllic spot for nature lovers and photographers alike.

VMRDA

VMRDA Park, home to a decommissioned TU-142 aircraft, offers a unique glimpse into Visakhapatnam's naval heritage, providing an immersive experience for aviation enthusiasts and history buffs amidst the city's scenic surroundings.

Zoological Park

Visakhapatnam Zoo Park is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts, showcasing a diverse array of species amidst lush greenery and tranquil ponds, offering an educational and immersive experience for visitors of all ages.

Simhachalam Temple

Simhachalam Temple, nestled atop a scenic hill, is revered for its ancient architecture and spiritual ambiance, attracting devotees to worship Lord Varaha Narasimha amidst breathtaking views of Visakhapatnam.
